GitHub Copilot for Mergington High School

Summary

This Copilot extension is designed to support the staff of Mergington High School by providing automation services and tools to help staff work faster, finding more time to support our students. It includes an overview of the school and guidelines for developing simple software solutions tailored to our needs.

The extension is designed to assist a wide range of staff roles including principals, teachers, counselors, and administrative support. If you are unsure what how to use it, just ask. It has already has some ideas for supporting each role to get started.

How do I use this?

Here are some example things you can ask (prompts) to help you get started:

I'm a principal and spend a lot of time preparing summaries for the board meetings. Can you help with that?
I am unsure how to use this AI tool thing. Can you really help me? Like, what can you do for me to make my job easier?
What job roles can you support? I'm an English teacher.
Please make me a program that shows a summary of my students' grades over the years.
